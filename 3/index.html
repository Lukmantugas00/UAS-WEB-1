Top Recipe of the Month: Ramen

This month's top recipe is a delightful bowl of ramen, featuring a rich broth, tender noodles, and a variety of delicious toppings.

Ingredients:

  • 200g fresh ramen noodles
  • 1 liter chicken or pork broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon miso paste
  • 2 boiled eggs, halved
  • 100g sliced pork belly
  • 1 sheet nori (seaweed), cut into strips
  • 1 green onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon chili oil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pot, bring the broth to a simmer. Add the soy sauce and miso paste, stirring until the miso is dissolved.
  3. Divide the cooked noodles into bowls. Pour the hot broth over the noodles.
  4. Top each bowl with halved boiled eggs, sliced pork belly, nori strips, green onion, sesame seeds, and chili oil if desired.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious bowl of ramen!

Tips:

  • For a richer broth, simmer the broth with pork bones for several hours.
  • Add vegetables like spinach, mushrooms, or bamboo shoots for extra flavor and nutrition.
  • Adjust the seasoning to your taste by adding more soy sauce or miso paste.

Serving suggestions:

Serve the ramen with a side of pickled ginger and a cup of green tea for a complete meal.

Nutritional information:

Each serving of ramen contains approximately 500 calories, 20g of protein, 60g of carbohydrates, and 20g of fat.
